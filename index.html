Saudi Arabia 2034

Congratulations to Saudi Arabia for their incredible accomplishment in hosting the FIFA world cup in 2034! Their win has made an impressive history showcasing their dedication and excellence.

Saudi vision 2030

Prior to Vision 2030, sports were not widely practiced in the kingdom. However, the vision has transformed this, encouraging active lifestyles and significantly increasing participation in sports. By building world-class facilities, supporting athletes, and hosting international events, Saudi Arabia has inspired its people to embrace sports and healthier living. This achievement reflects the incredible impact of those efforts.

What is Sport?

Sport is a physical activity involving skill, competition, and rules, played individually or whithin team setting for exercise, enetrtainment, or recreation, promoting health, discipline, and social interaction.

Problems in Why Citizens Don't Practice Sports

  • Lack of interest or preference for sports activities.
  • Traumatic experiences (e.g., injuries like a ball to the face or broken bones).
  • Loss of motivation to engage in physical activity.

Solutions

  1. Offering monetary rewards and gifts to outstanding players.
  2. Integrating physical education into schools, kindergartens, and universities.
  3. Promoting sports through online videos and distributing informative brochures.

The Benefits of Sports

  1. Decrease blood pressure in the human body.
  2. Reduce blood sugar levels.
  3. Help lower body fat and maintain a healthy weight.
  4. Help lower stress, anixiety, and depression, thus improving mood.

Recommendations

  1. Prioritize caring for your body as it is essential for survival.
  2. Teach time management to families for better health and lifestyle balance.
  3. Focus on three key factors of a healthy lifestyle:

Healthy Lifestyle Factors

  1. Getting enough sleep
  2. Eating a balanced diet
  3. Practicing regular physical activity
